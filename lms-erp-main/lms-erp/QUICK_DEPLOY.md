# ⚡ Super Quick Deploy (3 Minutes)

## 1-Click Railway Deployment

### 🎯 **Step 1: Click This Link**
[Deploy to Railway](https://railway.app/new/template?template=https://github.com/harigopalkonka/LMS_PROJECT)

### 🎯 **Step 2: Authorize GitHub**
- Click "Authorize railway-app"
- Select your `LMS_PROJECT` repository

### 🎯 **Step 3: Add Environment Variables**
In Railway dashboard, add these:

```
JWT_SECRET=your-secret-key-12345
```

### 🎯 **Step 4: Deploy**
- Click "Deploy Now"
- Wait 2-3 minutes
- **Your URL will appear here**: `https://your-app-name.railway.app`

---

## 🔐 Create Admin Account

After deployment, visit your app and:
1. Go to `/register`
2. Create account with email ending in `@admin.com`
3. The system will automatically make you an admin

---

## 🎉 You're Live!

Your LMS is now deployed at: `https://your-app-name.railway.app`
